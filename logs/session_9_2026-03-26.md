## Key Findings

1. **DPP9 tissue-specific KO data changes the safety narrative**: Non-inflammasome mechanisms (autophagy, p53) dominate in hepatocytes, suggesting fibroblast DPP9 biology may also be partly inflammasome-independent
2. **MUC1 functional evidence exceeds most higher-ranked targets**: KO protection + in vivo pharmacology + existing drug mechanism — only limited by subthreshold coloc
3. **NTN4 is entirely biologically distinct from NTN1**: Cannot extrapolate NTN1 pro-fibrotic findings
4. **DPP9 is the highest-connectivity network hub**: 3/6 pathway clusters connected
5. **Vascular cluster aligns with treprostinil's record IPF efficacy**

## Limitations

1. Hepatocyte ≠ fibroblast for DPP9 biology; 2. MUC1 coloc PP=0.616 subthreshold; 3. Pathway network manually curated; 4. NTN4 direction paradox unresolved; 5. No original computation
