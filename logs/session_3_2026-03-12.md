## Key New Findings

- **ATP11A has very strong colocalization** in smoker lung eQTL (PP.H4=0.996) and monocytes (PP.H4=0.994) — stronger than previously appreciated. This suggests ATP11A may be a better target than its modest blood coloc (0.621) suggested
- **DPP9 colocalizes specifically in fibroblasts** (PP.H4=0.989) — important for therapeutic strategy as fibroblasts are the key effector cells in IPF
- **PTPN9 and SNRPB2** are novel IPF survival genes identified by S-PrediXcan — these predict survival rather than susceptibility, which may be more clinically relevant
- **SMAD2** identified by TWAS is a core TGF-beta signaling effector — provides direct genetic support for the TGF-beta pathway in IPF
- **Nerandomilast (BI 1015550)** Phase 3 results published in NEJM (May 2025): 68.8 mL FVC benefit vs placebo at 52 weeks. Now approved. Still no direct genetic support for PDE4B target.
- **Multiple clinical trial failures**: galectin-3 inhibitor GB0139 and autotaxin inhibitor BBT-877 both failed Phase 2 — neither had genetic support
