## Key Findings

- IPF has **27 robust GWAS loci** spanning 6 biological pathways:
    - Telomere maintenance (TERT, TERC, OBFC1, RTEL1)
- Host defense / mucociliary (MUC5B, TOLLIP, FUT6)
    - Cell-cell adhesion (DSP, DPP9)
  - mTOR signaling (DEPTOR, NPRL3)
  - Spindle assembly (KIF15, KNL1, MAD1L1, STMN3)
  - Rho-GTPase / GPCR signaling (AKAP13, GPR157, RAPGEF2)
- **MUC5B rs35705950** is the dominant risk variant (OR=4.11, explains ~6-9% of liability)
- Several loci point to **druggable** targets: FKBP5 (immunophilin), DPP9 (protease), GPR157 (GPCR), PSKH1 (kinase)
- Partanen et al. 2022 multi-ancestry analysis revealed population-specific effects (PSKH1 in East Asians)

## Surprises

- The spindle assembly pathway is strongly implicated (4 genes: KIF15, KNL1, MAD1L1, STMN3) — unexpected for a fibrotic disease
- FKBP5 (immunophilin, stress-response protein targeted by FK506) is a novel IPF locus — potential for drug repurposing
- DEPTOR and NPRL3 both converge on mTOR pathway — rapamycin/rapalogs could be relevant

## Limitations

- Effect alleles may not be fully harmonized across studies
- The 17q21 KANSL1/MAPT locus was omitted from the CSV due to complexity of the inversion polymorphism
- Some novel loci from Allen 2022 with PPR < 90% were excluded
